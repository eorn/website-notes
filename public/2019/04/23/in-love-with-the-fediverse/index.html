In love with the Fediverse

Ewen · 2019/04/23 · 1 minute read

I’ve been aware of the Fediverse for some time already. Basically this is a “Federated Universe” of different social networks, based on open protocols. You might have heard of Mastodon for example.

Well, after reading an article about it (French version - Italian original version), I want to get involved in it.

This summer I’m planning on building a new hardware server, and I’ll try to set up instances of Mastodon, PeerTube and FunkWhale. I’m pretty excited about it huhu.
